About Me

Hi, I am Baturay Saglam, and I am a Master's student. Currently, I am pursuing my graduate studies at Bilkent University under the supervision of Prof. S. Serdar Kozat (http://www.ee.bilkent.edu.tr/~kozat/). I am a graduate of the Electrical and Electronics Engineering Department of Bilkent University with a GPA of 3.55/4.00. Moreover, I have been ranked 1390th among 2 Million students in the National University Entrance Exam.

I am currently part of an academic research team as a research assistant at Bilkent University Department of Electrical and Electronics Engineering (https://ee.bilkent.edu.tr/en///), where we conduct research on deep reinforcement learning algorithms and reinforcement learning theory.

My research interests include deep reinforcement learning algorithms, reinforcement learning theory, online learning, representation learning, deep learning, and interpretability in machine learning.
